Additional Cost Headers
Standardized cost line headers vendors populate during commercial bidding — enables accurate landed cost comparison across all vendors.
When Purchase creates an RFQ, they select which of these headers to expose to vendors. Vendors fill in their own values. The bid matrix auto-sums them into a landed cost per vendor, enabling L1/L2/L3 ranking.
