Add a Vendor
Vendor fills their own profile via a secure self-onboarding link
The vendor will receive an email with a unique onboarding link. They authenticate via Email + OTP, then fill in their GST number — the system auto-fetches company and PAN details from GSTN. Purchase reviews the completed profile before approval.
Vendor experience after receiving the link
1
Email + OTP verification
Vendor opens the unique link and verifies their email address via a one-time password
2
GST number entry → auto-fetch
System pulls company name, PAN, registered address, and business type automatically from GSTN
3
Profile completion
Vendor adds contact details, ownership structure, and uploads certificates & balance sheets
4
Purchase reviews & approves
Profile appears in your panel as "Pending Approval" — you verify and whitelist
